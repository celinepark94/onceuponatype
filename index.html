Hello and Welcome to
Once Upon a Type!

This is an ongoing project and learning experiment about typsetting. The stories listed below are works of fiction in the public domain, each typeset by a small team of budding type enthusiats. In formatting each story we experience firsthand the trials, tribulations, and joys, of typesetting onthe web.

This work is a product of Once Upon A Type, an online course from Hyperlink Academy.
